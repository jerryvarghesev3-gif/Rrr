Goal: Stakeholder & Service Engineer Engagement for Product Insight

WHAT

Strengthen engagement with service engineers and stakeholders to understand real-world product usage, field challenges, and improve delivery effectiveness.

⸻

HOW
	•	Engage with service engineers and field teams to understand real-time product usage and issues
	•	Participate in pilot site / field interactions to observe workflows and pain points
	•	Collaborate with PMO, support, and cross-functional teams for alignment
	•	Conduct periodic feedback sessions (e.g., quarterly syncs) with service teams
	•	Capture insights on field issues, usability gaps, and improvement areas
	•	Translate feedback into actionable development, debugging, or feature enhancements
	•	Share field learnings with the team to improve product reliability and supportability
	•	Contribute to improving service efficiency and issue resolution turnaround time
